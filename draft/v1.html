COMPANY PROFILE

PT. SANCHEM INDUSTRIAL PERTIWI is located at Desa Tobat, Balaraja - Tangerang. It can be reached within 1.5 hours ddrive by car from jakarta International Airport. The Factory was founded on June 2011, and since the beginning of establishment the factory has manufatured ink for Screen ink, Spray, Gravure ink for local and export market.
